# Instagram Story Design Research: Pixel-Perfect Standards

**Purpose:** Define standards for premium, non-templated Instagram Story images  
**Date:** 2025-11-16

---

## Instagram Story Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 1080px × 1920px (9:16 aspect ratio)
- **Safe Zone:** 1080px × 1420px (avoid top 250px and bottom 250px for UI elements)
- **File Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **File Size:** < 30MB (recommended < 5MB for fast loading)
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI minimum, 144 DPI recommended for retina

---

## Pixel-Perfect Design Principles

### 1. Typography Excellence

**Premium Characteristics:**
- Custom or distinctive typefaces (not system defaults)
- Proper kerning and tracking
- Optical alignment (not just mathematical centering)
- Hierarchy through size, weight, and spacing (not just bold/regular)
- Line height: 1.3-1.5 for body text, 1.1-1.2 for headlines

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Arial, Helvetica, Times New Roman (too generic)
- ❌ Comic Sans, Papyrus (unprofessional)
- ❌ More than 2-3 typeface families
- ❌ All caps for long text (reduces readability)

**Premium Typeface Options:**
- **Serif:** Playfair Display, Crimson Pro, Lora, Merriweather
- **Sans-serif:** Inter, Manrope, DM Sans, Space Grotesk, Outfit
- **Display:** Syne, Clash Display, Cabinet Grotesk

---

### 2. Color & Contrast

**Premium Characteristics:**
- Sophisticated color palettes (not primary colors)
- Proper contrast ratios (WCAG AA minimum: 4.5:1 for text)
- Subtle gradients (not harsh transitions)
- Consistent color temperature
- Strategic use of accent colors

**Color Psychology for Complaints:**
- Red: Urgency, importance (use sparingly)
- Blue: Trust, authority
- Gray: Neutrality, professionalism
- White: Clarity, space

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Pure black (#000000) - use rich blacks (#0a0a0a, #1a1a1a)
- ❌ Pure white (#ffffff) on bright backgrounds - use off-white (#fafafa, #f5f5f5)
- ❌ Neon colors (hard to read)
- ❌ Low contrast (accessibility issue)

---

### 3. Layout & Spacing

**Premium Characteristics:**
- Consistent spacing system (8px grid or 4px grid)
- Generous whitespace (breathing room)
- Optical balance (not just mathematical centering)
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Alignment to grid

**Golden Ratio:** 1.618 (use for proportions)
**Rule of Thirds:** Divide canvas into 3×3 grid, place key elements at intersections

**Spacing System:**
- Micro: 4px, 8px
- Small: 12px, 16px
- Medium: 24px, 32px
- Large: 48px, 64px
- XL: 80px, 96px

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Random spacing (use consistent system)
- ❌ Cramped layouts (text touching edges)
- ❌ Centered everything (boring, static)
- ❌ Inconsistent margins

---

### 4. Visual Elements

**Premium Characteristics:**
- Subtle shadows (not harsh drop shadows)
- Soft gradients (not banded)
- Rounded corners (modern, friendly)
- Layering and depth
- Strategic use of transparency

**Shadow Best Practices:**
- Use multiple shadows for depth (small + large)
- Blur radius: 20-60px for soft shadows
- Opacity: 10-30% for subtlety
- Y-offset: 10-40px for natural light

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Harsh drop shadows (looks dated)
- ❌ Overuse of effects (cluttered)
- ❌ Clipart or stock icons (generic)
- ❌ Busy backgrounds (distracting)

---

### 5. Content Hierarchy

**Premium Characteristics:**
- Clear focal point (what should user see first?)
- Size contrast: 3:1 ratio minimum between headline and body
- Weight contrast: Use font weights strategically
- Color contrast: Important elements stand out
- Spatial contrast: More space around important elements

**Visual Flow:**
1. Primary: Main message (largest, boldest)
2. Secondary: Supporting info (medium size)
3. Tertiary: Details (smallest, subtle)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Everything same size (no hierarchy)
- ❌ Too many focal points (confusing)
- ❌ Buried important info (poor UX)

## Premium Design Patterns for Complaint Images

### Pattern 1: Minimalist Card
- Large whitespace
- Single focal point (complaint text)
- Subtle branding
- Clean typography
- Soft shadows

### Pattern 2: Editorial Layout
- Magazine-style typography
- Strong hierarchy
- Pull quotes or callouts
- Professional color palette
- Grid-based layout

### Pattern 3: Modern Gradient
- Sophisticated gradient background
- Glassmorphism effects
- Layered content
- High contrast text
- Contemporary feel

### Typography
- [ ] Use premium typeface (not system default)
- [ ] Proper line height (1.3-1.5)
- [ ] Optical kerning enabled
- [ ] Clear hierarchy (3+ size levels)
- [ ] Readable at small sizes

### Color
- [ ] WCAG AA contrast ratio (4.5:1 minimum)
- [ ] Consistent color temperature
- [ ] Sophisticated palette (not primary colors)
- [ ] Strategic accent colors
- [ ] Rich blacks, off-whites

### Layout
- [ ] 8px grid system
- [ ] Generous whitespace
- [ ] Optical alignment
- [ ] Safe zone respected (avoid top/bottom 250px)
- [ ] Balanced composition

### Visual Quality
- [ ] Soft shadows (not harsh)
- [ ] Smooth gradients (not banded)
- [ ] Consistent border radius
- [ ] Proper layering
- [ ] No pixelation or artifacts

### Content
- [ ] Clear focal point
- [ ] Readable text (not too small)

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### Templated Look
- ❌ Canva-style layouts (overused)
- ❌ Generic stock photos
- ❌ Preset color schemes
- ❌ Default fonts
- ❌ Obvious templates

### Poor Quality
- ❌ Pixelated text
- ❌ Banded gradients
- ❌ Harsh shadows
- ❌ Low contrast
- ❌ Cramped spacing

### Unprofessional
- ❌ Comic Sans or similar
- ❌ Too many effects
- ❌ Clipart
- ❌ Neon colors
- ❌ All caps everywhere

## Recommended Approach for Bijak Mengeluh

### Typography
- **Headline:** Inter Bold (48-56px) - modern, professional
- **Body:** Inter Regular (32-36px) - readable, clean
- **Agency:** Inter SemiBold (28-32px) - emphasis
- **Branding:** Inter ExtraBold (28px) - distinctive

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** #dc2626 (red-600) - urgency, action
- **Background:** Linear gradient #fafafa → #f5f5f5 (subtle)
- **Text:** #1a1a1a (rich black) - readable
- **Accent:** #991b1b (red-800) - depth
- **Card:** #ffffff with soft shadow

### Layout
- **Safe zone:** 80px padding top/bottom, 60px left/right
- **Card:** Rounded 32px corners, soft shadow
- **Spacing:** 8px grid system
- **Hierarchy:** 48px headline, 32px body, 28px agency

### Visual Style
- **Shadow:** 0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- **Gradient:** Subtle, 5-10% opacity difference
- **Border radius:** 24-32px for cards
- **Transparency:** 95-98% for overlays
